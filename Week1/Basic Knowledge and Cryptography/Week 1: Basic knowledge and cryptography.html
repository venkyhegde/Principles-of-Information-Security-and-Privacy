I. Computer Security: Lightweight Introduction




Cyberspace includes only the Infrastructure but it doesn’t include the information in it. In PISP we protect two things.
  1. Infrastructure
  2. Data in it (Data in the infrastructure)





Crypto locker - Breaking into computer and encrypt all our files  and ask for ransom. 

Attackers can do in two ways
  1. IP address spoofing - using other people ip address to send some packets/something but it will not become interactive. (One way)
  2. Breaking into someone else computer to take control and stay at low.
